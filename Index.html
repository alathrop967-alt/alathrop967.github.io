What is Slay the Spire?

Slay the Spire is a deck-building rougelike game, available on most platforms.


In Slay the Spire, you play through three different "acts" (levels), fighting different enemies and bosses as you climb the spire. You can choose to play as four different characters: the Ironclad, the Silent, the Defect, or the Watcher. Each of these characters has unique cards and relics, allowing for a plethora of replayability.
