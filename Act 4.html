Act 4: The Ending

Act 4 (offically known as the The Ending) is the true final act and is the most unique of all the acts. It is the only Act with only 1 path available, and has the same path every time, that being a campfire, merchant, elite fight, and the boss. To reach Act 4, you must gather the three keys that represent the ironclad (red key-obtained at campfire), the silent (green key-obtained by fighting a burning elite), and the defect (blue key-obtained at a chest). The watcher is not included because she was added after Act 4. Beating Act 4 with different characters gives you a cutscene, as well as a message from Neow, the giant whale that revives you after every death.


The Spire Shield and Spear is one of the toughest fights in the game, as well as one of the most unique. They have you surrounded, with the enemy behind you doing 50% more damage (you can change directions with attacks or targetted potions). Turn one is pretty tame, with a minor amount of damage, but turn two is really scary. The reason you take so little damage on turn one is because they are adding two burns to the top of your draw pile, and hitting you for 40 damage and 30 damage respectively. If you have no additonal draw or an inconsitant deck, this means that you will have to block 40+ damage with only 3 random cards (2 if you draw ascenders bane [an unremovable curse added to your deck at higher asceions] or a different curse). Generally, you want to save a potion for turn two of this fight, such as a swiftness potion (draw 3) to help offset the 2 dead draws on turn two. After you burst down one, it becomes a much easier fight, though it is still easy to lose a lot of health and go into the heart fight with next to no health.
